Ante
Ryan Untalan
Jun. 5, 2025 · 5 min
Summary
Inspiration: I remember seeing a funny video where someone asked ChatGPT, What should I name my son? be creative. That video was clearly a joke, but it stuck with me. It made me think about how much we now rely on generative AI to write and even think for us. That constant urge to "just ask AI" is what sparked this project: Ante.
Project Description: A writing space free from autocomplete and AI suggestions. Just you and your thoughts. And a community of writers to inspire one another.
Role: Designer & Developer
Tools: Figma, Bolt, Supabase, Netlify
Duration: 5 days
Demo
Within 5 days, I designed, built, and deployed Ante for the world's largest hackathon presented by Bolt. Ante is a featured project on Bolt's gallery page.
