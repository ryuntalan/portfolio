Project Process
EMPATHIZE
Interviews

My team and I conducted two interviews with teachers to gain a deeper understanding of our potential users. We developed a questionnaire as a guide for our interview and it was conducted through video call. One key question we asked was "If you had an application that can help you with anything with teaching, what would it do and why?" Some key insights that we gained from this question was "ensuring that lesson plans align with common core standards" and ""something that maintains student engagement and encourages participation among students."

Online Research

We also explored the internet to gain more information on the A&E Values (owners of thr HISTORY channel), pain points in teaching history, and statistics that correspond to our user needs. One interesting statistic that we found was about how student engagement drops each school year.

"The Gallup Student Poll surveyed nearly 500,000 students in grades five through 12 from more than 1,700 public schools in 37 states in 2012. They found that nearly eight in 10 elementary students who participated in the poll are engaged with school. By middle school that falls to about six in 10 students. And by high school, only four in 10 students qualify as engaged (Gallup Student Polls, 2012)."
